Spotify Music Fingerprint

Key

Acousticness: is the track acoustic?
Danceability: how suitable for dancing?
Energy: perceptual measure of intensity and activity (Bach vs Death Metal)
Instrumentalness: does the track contain any vocals? (instrumental above .5)
Liveness: is the audience in the recording?
Speechiness: are there spoken words? (audiobook)
Valence: higher valence = more positive track
